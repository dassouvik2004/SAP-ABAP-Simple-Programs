* Root of numbers
DATA: NUMS TYPE I, RESULT TYPE I.

  NUMS   = SY-INDEX.
  RESULT = SY-INDEX * NUMS.
  WRITE: / SY-INDEX,'x', NUMS, '=', RESULT.
ENDDO.
